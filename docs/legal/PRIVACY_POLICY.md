## 2. Roles: controller vs processor

### 2.1 Provider as controller

We act as **controller** for personal data we decide **how and why** to process, for example:

- Account and billing records;
- Service operations, security, fraud prevention;
- Product analytics **about use of the Service** (in line with this Policy);
- Marketing **where permitted** by law;
- Support communications with you.

### 2.2 Provider as processor (B2B workspaces)

When a **Customer organization** uses Tymio and its Users submit **business content** (e.g. initiatives, comments, CRM-style records) that contain personal data about **employees, contractors, or third parties**, the **Customer** is typically the **controller** of that personal data in the **workspace**. We process it as **processor** on the Customer’s instructions, subject to our **Data Processing Agreement (DPA)** **[URL or “available on request”]**.

### 2.3 B2Vibecoders

If you are an **individual** workspace owner without a separate corporate controller relationship, we are generally **controller** for **account** data and **processor-like** obligations still apply to **your workspace content** as described in your agreement with us; in practice, **you** decide what you store in the product.
