## 2) Priority Framework Agreed in Meeting

Use this framework to evaluate every backlog item:

- **Impact on patient and doctor outcomes** (clinical and operational value)
- **Revenue/cost effect** (new revenue, retention, efficiency, reduced manual load)
- **Risk reduction** (compliance, security, reliability)
- **Delivery complexity** (dependencies, architecture impact, team effort)
- **Time-to-value** (can we deliver meaningful value quickly?)

Decision model:

- `P0` = Must do now (critical for strategy, compliance, or stability)
- `P1` = Should do next (high value, medium urgency)
- `P2` = Could do later (important but not urgent)
- `P3` = Nice to have / discovery
